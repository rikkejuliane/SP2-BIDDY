The Idea Sparks

Biddy was born from a simple idea: to create a marketplace that prioritizes trust and ease of use. Frustrated with complicated selling platforms, our founders envisioned a space where anyone could list and buy items effortlessly.

Building the Foundation

With a small but dedicated team, development on Biddy officially began. We focused on building a user-friendly platform with strong security measures to ensure safe transactions for all users.

The Launch

Biddy officially launched, welcoming its first users. The response was overwhelming! Early adopters loved the simplicity of buying and selling, and we quickly gained traction in the marketplace space.

1 Million Listings!

Two years after launch, Biddy hit a huge milestone: over **1 million listings**! Our community grew stronger, with thousands of buyers and sellers trusting Biddy for their transactions.

The Future of Biddy

Today, Biddy is more than just a marketplace—it’s a thriving community. With innovative features, enhanced security, and a growing user base, we’re excited to continue shaping the future of online buying and selling.
